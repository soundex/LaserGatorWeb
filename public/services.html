7140-1 FAA Applications and Coordination

Outdoor laser displays that may affect airspace require FAA applications and coordination. We prepare and manage FAA Form 7140-1 submissions, NOTAMs, and related planning so your outdoor production stays compliant and on schedule.

  • Airspace and venue location review
  • FAA Form 7140-1 application preparation and filing
  • FAA NOTAM filing and laser display notification
  • Outdoor beam termination and scan-angle planning
  • Coordination with show producers, venues, and FAA contacts
