About LaserGator

Passion for light, music, and live performance since the 1980s.

Our Story

It started in 8th grade — a fascination with light, music, and the magic of Laserium shows in the 1980s. What began as wonder at a planetarium laser concert became a lifelong pursuit of the art and science of laser entertainment.

For over 13 years, LaserGator has been producing professional laser shows across the Pacific Northwest and beyond. From intimate club venues to large outdoor festivals, we bring the same passion and precision to every production.

Today, LaserGator combines decades of experience with modern laser technology, safety certification, and creative vision to deliver shows that audiences remember long after the last beam fades.
